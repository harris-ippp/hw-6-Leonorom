Why the name

When we adopted Clara we were told her name was Greta, but we didn't think it suited her so we renamed her.

Clara means light or white in spanish. It is also a female name, very common in Spain but not so much in Mexico were Clara was born.

We chose the name because it was short and cute, but also refers to how she looks.

How she came into my life

After our weiner dog passed away we thought we wouldn't get any other pets for a very long time. However a year after that, I saw an ad for Jack Russell puppies and decided to call just to see how what was the price. I had heard that Jack Russels are very expensive in Mexico and was just very curious.

The lady that picked up the phone told me that they only had one puppie left but because it was already 4 months old nobody would buy it and they were going to put it in a shelter. I decided it was a sign and told the lady we could keep it!
